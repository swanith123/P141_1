AI TABLE TENNIS




Status:




Game Creator: Swanith Varma Vuppalapati

Instructions

  1. First keep you laptop screen straight.
  2. Move yourself approximately 3-4 feet away from the device.
  3. Move your right wrist in an ascending and descending manner, so a red dot would appear on your right wrist.
  4. Now press te play button, and again move yourself 3-4 feet away from your device.
  5. The red paddle is your paddle, which will move as per the movement of your right wrist.
